# ERIL Governance Scope (Public)

## What ERIL Governs

ERIL governs **how** decisions are executed, not **what** decisions should be made.

Specifically, ERIL governs:
- admissibility of inputs,
- evaluation order,
- refusal conditions,
- evidence generation,
- and replay verification.

---

## What ERIL Does Not Govern

ERIL does not:
- define policy intent,
- judge outcome quality,
- assert legal compliance,
- or replace institutional accountability.

Those responsibilities remain with the adopting organization.

---

## Scope Boundary

This repository demonstrates **single‑decision ERIs** with intentionally narrow scope.

Multi‑system orchestration, cross‑domain governance, and enterprise lifecycle management are out of scope for the public reference surface.
